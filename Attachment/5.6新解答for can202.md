## Part I: DSB-SC AM System Analysis

### a) Selection and Justification of Carrier Frequency $f_{c1}$
The carrier frequency is selected as **$f_{c1} = 20,000$ Hz**.
- **Prevention of Spectral Overlap:** $f_{c1} \gg B_m$ (where $B_m \approx 4.5$ kHz).
- **Sampling Rate:** Upsampled to $200$ kHz to avoid aliasing.

### c) Coherent Detection Derivation
1. **Multiplication:** $2s_{DSB}(t)\cos(2\pi f_{c1}t) = 2m(t)\cos^2(2\pi f_{c1}t)$
2. **Trigonometric Identity:** $m(t) + m(t)\cos(4\pi f_{c1}t)$
3. **Filtering:** An FIR LPF recovers $m(t)$.

### d) Carrier Mismatch
Result: $y_{rec}(t) \approx m(t)\cos(2\pi \Delta f t + \theta)$.
- **$\Delta f$:** Causes "beating" effect (volume fluctuation).
- **$\theta$:** Causes constant amplitude attenuation.

## Part II: FM System Analysis

### f) Bandwidth Derivation
- **Carson's Rule:** $B_{FM} \approx 2(\Delta f + B_m) \approx 46.48$ kHz.
- **95% Power Bandwidth:** Measured at $\approx 16.36$ kHz.

## Part III: Noise Performance
FM (SNR $\approx 20$ dB) outperforms AM (SNR $\approx 14$ dB) by trading bandwidth for SNR gain and using a hard limiter.
